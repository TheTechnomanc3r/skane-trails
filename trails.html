Top Trails of Skåne


1.Skånelden

Skåneleden is the most famous and popular trail in skåne. It's a fairly long trail that passes trough alot of skåne and covers all kind of environments, spanning from forests,valleys all the way to coastal areas.

2.Kullaleden

Kullaleden is a favorite amongst those who want to hike both in a forest and coastal environment. This is seen as a revigorating hike and the fresh air breeze from the ocean is a nice bonus.

3.Mälarhusens strand

In the southeast Skåne you will find this peaceful trail that stretches across coastal areas and large beaches. A good start for beginners and also a perfect place to take a bath if you are visiting during the summer months.

4.Klöva Hallar

Klöva Hallar is like a landscape taken from a fantasy novel. You can find deep valleys,forests and even rivers troughout this trail. A highly recommended trail to relieve some stress and let the mind wonder.

5.Hilleshögs dalar

This is your typical valley hike with large open areas and pastures. A great view where you likely will encounter som wildlife awell. The trail also stretches down to the beach and cover some coastal areas too.
