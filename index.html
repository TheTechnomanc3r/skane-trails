Explore the beautiful nature of Skåne

Hiking Adventures

In southern Sweden you fill find this beatiful county famous for it's vast fields, deep forests and overall pleasent scenery. Do you like the coastal environments, the magnificent forests or maybe the stunning open valleys? There is no wrong place to start your hike. Young or old? Beginner or Expert. The trails of skåne offers something for everyone So get your boots on and start hiking

Recommended Equipment

Some trails can involve som rougher terrain and the weather can shift a lot depending on the time of year you visit. The first thing you should make sure you have are a sturdy pair of hiking boots. Some other things that come in handy are Backpack / Water / Powerbank-Solarcharger for your phone.

Good to know

The trails can vary alot depending on the season. Some trails are best to experience during the summer season and some are alot less populated during the winter season. There can also be animal areas connected to the trails, It's recommended to keep your pets on a leash whilst hiking on such trails.
